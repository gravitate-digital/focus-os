Security and Privacy
Your data stays on this device
Focus stores everything in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server, database, or third party. No cookies. No tracking. No analytics.
App links open external sites (Asana, Google, etc.) in new tabs. Those sites have their own login and security. Focus does not access, read, or store your credentials for any external service.
Asana task sync is not available in this version. Pulling tasks from Asana requires OAuth authentication with a backend server to handle tokens securely. This will be available when Focus moves to a proper app build (Next.js + Supabase). For now, tasks are managed manually within Focus.
To clear all data: use the Reset button below, or run localStorage.removeItem('focos_v10') in the browser console.
